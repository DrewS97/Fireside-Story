It was a dark and stormy night...

There was a disturbing chill in the air and not a sound was heard amidst the tempest, until...

A hole opened up in the clouds the size of a city and...

Objects small and large started pouring down, raining destruction upon the landscape. One of these mysterious objects landed so close I could just make it out...

A glowing rock? As the landscape became more and more littered with embers I questioned whether I should investigate or stay in the "safety" of my home...
